Low-carbon

Our events are low-carbon by design. Our aim is to leave only footprints...

The biggest carbon impact is travel to the event, therefore...

  • Please do NOT travel to the event in a car (unless you need to for accessibility reasons).

The events are aimed at people that are within 'self-powered distance' of the sites.

Upcoming Events

Allerton

International Dawn Chorus Day (Reveille 2023)

Sunday 7th May 2023

Chellow Dean

Twilight: 04:37
Sun Rise: 05:20

Meet 4:15am at The Fleece Inn at the corner of Allerton Road and North Parade, BD15 7DY.

Listen Live

We'll walk as a group to Chellow Dean via North Parade, with our walk leader Chris, to a spot in the woods on the edge of Chellow Dean.

When we reach our place in the woods we will stay there until the sun has risen (5:20am) and witness the dawning of the day.

We will broadcast the sounds we hear as part of Reveille 2023, an international broadcast of the Dawn Chorus from around the globe.

We anticipate that people will make their own creative responses to the event through drawing, writing, poetry, and so on.

Once the sun has risen our walk leader will guide people back to the Fleece Inn.

Please Note
  • You will arrive in the dark, before the sun rises. Bring a torch.
  • Invitations for this event have been distributed to people living within walking distance of the site.
  • In order to keep this a low carbon event we would ask that people do not travel by car.
  • The event will take place whatever the weather - unless there is a severe weather warning.
  • You will need to be self-sufficient for about 2 hours. You are responsible for your own welfare at this event.
  • There are no amenities e.g. toilets, shelter, shops, or car parks, at these sites.
You might bring
  • A torch - both to see and to be seen.
  • Appropriate clothing for the weather and terrain. Walking boots, water proof clothing, spare layers, hat, gloves.
  • An umbrella.
  • You may be stationary, outside for about an hour. Some people may bring sleeping bags.
  • Food and drink.
  • Something to sit on. A camping/carry mat or a fold up chair.
  • An audio recording device (a phone would do).
  • Notepad and pen for writing or drawing.
  • Your creative craft/activity.
