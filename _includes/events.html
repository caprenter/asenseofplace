The biggest carbon impact is travel to the event, therefore...

  • Please do NOT travel to the event in a car (unless you need to for accessibility reasons).

The events are aimed at people that are within 'self-powered distance' of the sites.

What you need to know

  • You will arrive in the dark, before the sun rises.
  • The event will take place whatever the weather - unless there is a severe weather warning.
  • You will need to be self-sufficient for about 2 hours. You are responsible for your own welfare at this event.
  • There are no amenities e.g. toilets, shelter, shops, or car parks, at these sites.
You might bring
  • A torch - both to see and to be seen.
  • Appropriate clothing for the weather and terrain. Walking boots, water proof clothing, spare layers, hat, gloves.
  • An umbrella.
  • You may be stationary, outside for about an hour. Some people may bring sleeping bags.
  • Food and drink.
  • Something to sit on. A camping/carry mat or a fold up chair.
  • An audio recording device (a phone would do).
  • Notepad and pen for writing or drawing.
  • Your creative craft/activity.
